FAQ

Everything you need
to know.

How long does it take to get my first video? +
Upload your photos and Xelo delivers both the MLS and social video within 9–12 minutes. No editing, no approvals — it's ready to publish.
Do I need to provide my own drone footage for aerial video? +
No drone needed. Xelo uses Google Aerial View satellite footage and AI-generated flyovers — all pulled automatically from the property address. When you request an aerial view, Xelo also shows the relevant EU drone permit requirements for your country, so you know the rules if you ever want to supplement with your own footage.
Which languages does Xelo support? +
9 European languages: English, Dutch, German, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, Polish, and Swedish. Both the narration (TTS voice) and the listing copy are generated in your chosen language. James AI responds in whatever language the buyer writes in — even if it's not on this list.
What is Xelo Market and do I have to pay extra for it? +
Xelo Market is included in every paid plan. It's a European property portal where every listing has a professional video. Your listings are published automatically when you create them — no extra upload. It's the only portal with video-first search and James AI for buyer enquiries.
What are "credits" and how do they work? +
Credits are the internal unit Xelo uses to generate video. One full listing video pack (MLS 16:9 + Social 9:16) costs 110 credits. Your plan includes a monthly credits allowance. Credits accumulate — they never expire. If you run out, you can buy a top-up without upgrading your plan.
Can I use Xelo on my existing website? +
Yes. The embed widget lets you add a Xelo video player to any website with one line of HTML. Your listings already live on Xelo Market — the embed just mirrors them on your own site. No API key or backend needed.
